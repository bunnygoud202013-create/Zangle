About Zangle Technologies

Business reform involves restructuring operations, optimizing workflows, and implementing modern strategies to improve efficiency and profitability. It focuses on leveraging new technologies and innovative practices to stay competitive. Zangle Technologies is committed to providing cutting-edge IT solutions, helping businesses embrace digital transformation through advanced software, AI-powered applications, and expert IT consulting.

Company History

  • 2021
  • 2022
  • 2023
  • 2024

Zangle Technologies was founded in November 2021 in Cumming, Georgia, with the goal of delivering innovative IT staffing and software development services. The company began its journey by collaborating with startups and enterprises to provide custom software solutions. Within its first year, Zangle Technologies established a strong foundation, focusing on research and development to identify key areas of growth in the IT industry. The company also initiated partnerships with local tech firms to enhance its service offerings.

In 2022, Zangle Technologies expanded its service offerings to include AI-powered applications and advanced data analytics, helping businesses streamline operations and improve decision-making. The company also secured key partnerships with global tech firms, allowing it to work on large-scale enterprise projects. Zangle introduced cloud computing solutions, enabling companies to migrate to scalable and secure cloud infrastructure. The company also expanded its workforce, recruiting top talent in AI, cybersecurity, and software engineering.

By 2023, Zangle Technologies had successfully implemented cloud-based infrastructure solutions and cybersecurity enhancements for its clients. The company saw significant growth in demand for its enterprise-level IT solutions, leading to the opening of a new research and development center. Zangle also ventured into blockchain technology, developing secure and transparent digital solutions for businesses. Additionally, the company launched several training programs to upskill its employees and keep them updated with the latest technological advancements.

By 2024, Zangle Technologies had solidified its position as a leader in IT solutions, supporting various industries, filing multiple H-1B visa applications to hire top-tier talent, and expanding its operations to international markets. The company continues to innovate with emerging technologies such as blockchain and AI-driven automation. Zangle also introduced a new AI-driven customer support system, improving service efficiency for its clients. The company further expanded its reach by opening regional offices in Europe and Asia, ensuring a global presence and accessibility to businesses worldwide.
